Dataset 1:-
It has total 5 features from which 4 are independent feature and one is dependent one. Independent variables are R&D Spend, Administration, Marketing Spend and State whereas dependent feature is how much profit company has made. Dataset consist of total 50 companies records but the name of the company has been confidential.
In this we have to prepare a model in which on the basis of 4 variables we have to check:-
1) impact of each variable on the dependent variable.
2) prediction of profit on the basis of expenditure in R&D, Administration and Marketing with the state in which expenditure has happende.
